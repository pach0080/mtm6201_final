Our Adoption Process Explained.

At Pawsitive Future, the safety and well-being of our pets are our top priorities. Our adoption process ensures that every animal finds a loving and suitable home where they will thrive, while also creating a fulfilling experience for the adopter. Here's how we do it:


Application Review
We carefully review each adoption application to understand the potential adopter’s lifestyle and environment, ensuring a perfect match between the pet and the home.


Meet & Greet
We arrange a meet and greet to allow both the adopter and the pet to interact in a safe, calm setting. This step ensures that the animal feels comfortable and that the adopter feels confident in their choice.


Home Visit/Interview
For added assurance, we may conduct a home visit or a detailed interview to ensure the living environment is suitable for the pet’s needs, with safety precautions in place.


Adoption Agreement
Once the adoption is confirmed, we establish an agreement that outlines both the responsibilities of the adopter and the care requirements of the pet to ensure long-term happiness and well-being.
