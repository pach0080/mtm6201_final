Whether you're joining us at an event or volunteering your time, every effort helps create a brighter future for pets in need. Your involvement brings us closer to giving every animal the care and love they deserve. Together, we can make a lasting impact—one paw at a time.



Paws for a Cause:
Charity Walk – Walk for Rescued Pets

Join us for a fun-filled day of walking with your furry friend! The Paws for a Cause Charity Walk is a 5k event where all proceeds go toward supporting Pawsitive Future's mission to rescue and rehome pets in need. Bring your dogs, your family, and your friends for a heartwarming day of exercise and community.

Next event: April 11th, 2025



Bark & Brew:
Pet-Friendly Happy Hour – Sip, Relax & Support

Come enjoy a relaxing evening at our Bark & Brew event, where you can sip on local craft beers and mingle with fellow animal lovers. It’s the perfect opportunity to meet new people, make new furry friends, and support Pawsitive Future—while enjoying a pet-friendly venue.
